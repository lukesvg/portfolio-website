DSIGN® - Design Resources E-Commerce Platform"

DSIGN® is a conceptual e-commerce store giving designers access to premium resources and assets, inside a beautiful, user-friendly interface. The DSIGN® interface contains all the necessary features and functions a modern e-commerce store should consist of, including stylish, functional product cards, easy to use filtering options, a “Saved” section and a “Browse” section for viewing a range of categories useful for the modern designer. For this project, I aimed to design a modern, sleek looking interface that was also highly user-friendly. This involved taking inspiration from various popular e-commerce stores to design a unique but familiar interface that would give users a smooth experience when interacting with the website.

My Process

Research

Research for this project included competitor analysis, moodboards and user testing. Using the information from the project brief, I came up with my idea and began reviewing similar offerings in the market, making note of the design, user experience and product goals in my notes. I then began building up a moodboard of designs, screenshots, landing pages, product imagery and other material to begin forming a moodboard to inform and gather inspiration for the project. Following the design of my wireframes, I prototyped them and sent the prototype to a range of users asking questions to help gather feedback that could me iterate on the design.

Ideation

My idea for the project was formed quite early on, so the main ideation phase was focused around the feedback I recieved from my wireframe prototype. Using this feedback, I decided to remove the sidebar, updated my filter system and resized and restructured my card system for the product tiles.

Wireframes

I sketched some rough idea on paper, then designed in Figma a set of 5 screens in grayscale to begin formulating the overall layout, look and feel of the product.
